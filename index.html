About Our Mission

Why Say No to Drugs?

Drug abuse impacts millions annually, causing physical harm, mental health challenges, fractured relationships, and lost potential. Our mission is to empower individuals with education, support, and resources to make informed choices and resist substance abuse.

We champion prevention, early intervention, and community support to foster a healthier, drug-free society.

Helplines & Support

National Helplines

  • Drug Abuse Hotline: 1-800-662-HELP (4357)

    Available 24/7, 365 days a year

  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741

    Free 24/7 support from trained crisis counselors

  • Youth Helpline: 1-800-448-4663

    Specialized support for teens and young adults

Police Assistance

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please call emergency services:

Emergency: 911

For immediate life-threatening situations

For non-emergency police assistance or to report drug-related activities:

  • Drug Tip Line: 1-800-222-TIPS (8477)
  • Anonymous Online Reporting: www.tipsubmit.com
